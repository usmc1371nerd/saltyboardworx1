This is a U.S. Veteran started and ran company. The owner James Dumas is a orginally from the salty breezy little town of Panama City Beach. Mostly being a tourist town and a hot spot for family vacations. He start out crafting and wood working at a very young age. His inspirition to working with wood, was his grandfather a master carpenter. He followed along his grandfather in most of his home projects learning. His grandfather would take his time teaching him woodcraft and carpentry of all shorts. His grandfather having a vast amount of knowledge of all types of wood construction skills he would spend days upon days just soaking up knowledge. This lead up to his adulthood where he eventually joined "The Few, The Proud,"The Marines and served a honroable 8 years. During his time in The Marines he was a combat engineer and still continued to build his skill set for wood construction. While in the Marines he deployed for the global war on terrorism to Afghanistan , and Iraq. After his time and tours in he Marines he pursued all kinds of career paths but some how always leading back woodworking of some shorts. He has built over 30 homes working for various companies. Also starting his own handyman business and closing it a few years later after the COVId epidemic. He now resides in Birmingham, Al where he has started a custom woodworking business that provides custom wood working pieces to anyone and everyone. .
